# Tester invite

Subject: Quick feedback on a small Apple/Mac project?

Hi,

I’m putting together a small public project called Take Back the Mac:

https://takebackthemac.org/testers/

The basic argument is that Apple has enough control over the Mac, iPhone, iPad, App Store, background behavior, and app installation/removal that it should use that control more clearly in the user’s interest. Clean uninstall, visible app footprints, honest background behavior, local ownership, and a better App Store should be table stakes for devices people paid for.

I’m trying to turn a cranky but real feeling into something specific, useful, and fair. If you have a few minutes, I’d love feedback on:

- Whether the manifesto feels clear and worth sharing.
- Which requests feel strongest or weakest.
- What important request is missing.
- Whether the tone is sharp enough without becoming lazy Apple-bashing.
- Any examples, screenshots, or stories that would make the argument stronger.

You can upvote requests, add a new request, or leave comments on the site. New requests and comments are reviewed before they appear publicly. Email is optional and only for follow-up if you want that.

Thanks for taking a look.

Paul
